FAQS

In what shape and form can I craft my messages?

If you're visiting a Post Office or a Mahindra dealership, letters, poems, and sketches on paper of any type will work fine; small artefacts; and small embroidery items also work fine.

What is the maximum permissible size and weight of the article that I can deposit?

The message or article should not be bigger than 13 cm x 20 cm x 1.5 cm in size and should not weigh more than 250 gm.

Where do I go to deposit my message or article?

You may visit the nearest Mahindra Authorized showroom or workshop to hand over their message or article. You may visit https://auto.mahindra.com/dealer-locator to locate the nearest Mahinda showroom or workshop. Alternatively, they may call our With You Hamesha number 1800-209-6006 to know the same. You may also put up your message on social media platforms with #HeartsForBravehearts

When can I go to the nearest Mahindra showroom or workshop to deposit my message or article?

You may visit anytime 7th of June 2024 onwards

When is the last date before which I may visit the nearest Mahindra showroom or workshop to deposit my message or article?

You may visit anytime before the 23rd of June 2024.

How can I share my messages with Mahindra online?

You may post your message on Instagram, Facebook, X or YouTube with #HeartsForBravehearts and tag us.
